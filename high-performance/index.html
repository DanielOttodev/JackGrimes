About Us

Welcome To JJ's High Performance

My name is Jack Grimes. I played for the Melbourne Football Club for 9 years, including a 3 year stint as co-captain. Whilst playing I opened my own personal training studio, coaching everyone from first timers to aspiring pro athletes.

After a stint at local footy, when my AFL days came to and end, I realised there’s a huge gap between the local and elite level when it comes to strength & conditioning. Way bigger than it should be.

So I started developing a platform that would change the game at local level for players wanting to reach their full potential. One that would be affordable, easy to use and allow them to fit it around work, school or uni.

I’ve been lucky to have learnt from some of the best strength & conditioning coaches in the country. But I’ve also lent on the experience of those around me to put this together, including my brother Dylan who had a very impressive career at the Richmond FC after 3 premierships & a B&F over 13 years.

Through the JJ’s app, I’ll not just tell you what to do but I'll coach you through it .

Jack Grimes

Founder & Coach

STRENGTH

Football specific programs tailored to your and your experience level, schedule, and goals. Whether your focus is gaining some muscle mass, improving your power & explosiveness, or a combination, you’ll have a program to suit. I’ll coach you through it with 100’s of exercise demos, and full step by step instructions to make sure your technique is on-point. Record every set, rep & workout through the app, and track your progress as you move through the training phases.

RUNNING

Through the JJ’s app, I’ll coach you though a range of short and sharp, football specific sessions to take your ability to get from contest to contest to a new level. All tailored to you and your ability, strengths and weaknesses. Again, you’ll be guided with my step by step video demos, and full written descriptions.

SKILLS

Skills will be a part of almost every running session, and it’s not just running through the motions. It’s intense, and it’s with a purpose. Through the app, you’ll be guided through some technical skills, some at game-like intensity, and some under real fatigue so your skills are ready to stand up under game day pressure. Solo skills are also available for those who don’t have a partner.

NUTRITION

JJ’s flexible meal plans will give you the control.
With access to a huge database of healthy recipes to make sure you’re ticking all those boxes. You mix and match your meals as you like, knowing it’s all right for you and your needs. That coupled with weight tracking, body measurement and composition check ins to make sure the rig is on track.
